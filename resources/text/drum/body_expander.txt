Drum body expander machine hydraulically operated with 7.5 H.P. Power pack and hydraulic accessories. Machine having two segments expanded through taper cone system connected to hydraulic cylinder through bush system and retracted through internal spring system. Segments duly hard chromed, segment assembly fitted on sturdy M.S. Fabricated stand. Machine vertical and drum shell to be fed manually in the machine. Machine operated vide panel control system, electrical and hydraulic accessories. Two guide plates provide on two sides of segments for additional support. Complete with standard fittings and accessories.

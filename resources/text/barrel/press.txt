Press type Flanging machine for Barrels with a 5 H.P.hydraulic power pack and hydraulic Accessories. Machine made of sturdy M.S. plates construction and hydraulic cylinder fitted on top of machine and connected to flanging die dishes through guide bush assembly and four nos. sturdy M.S. shafts or pillars in between the two plates to make the machine very heavy and rigid in construction. Machine operated vide panel control system and electrical accessories.Machine provided with two nos, triple seam dies and one open top die dish,complete with standard accessories.

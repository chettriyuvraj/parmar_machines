De Greasing unit for Barrels, M.S. fabricated and rectangular type structure. De greasing unit having to separate tanks for water and cleaning chemicals and fitted with two Nos.2 H.P. electrical pumps respectively. Water and chemicals are pumped from the tank and sprayed with force through net work of pipelines and nozzles on Barrels to clean them. Barrels Entering and exiting the unit vide gravity conveyor system. Complete with standard fittings.

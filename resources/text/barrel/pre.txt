Pre Curling machine, to pre curl top and bottom lids of Barrel for triple seam. Machine M.S, Fabricated and having two Nos. geared motors, one to drive the chuck spindle and one to drive the cam assembly. One geared motor is 3 H.P. and the other 2 H.P respectively. Machine having a pneumatic clutch and brake assembly and operated vide panel control system and  accessories Lid to be manually inserted in machine after which the machine pre curls the lid and ejects it as the machine is sloped. Complete with one set roller duly hardened and standard fittings.
